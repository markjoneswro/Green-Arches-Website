Green Arches Apartment

We are located on the first floor of a newly constructed building in Łagów at 20D Chopina Street.

The apartment is well positioned for exploring Łagów and the surrounding natural areas. The lake, forest trails, and local attractions are all within easy reach, making it a convenient and peaceful base for both relaxation and outdoor activities.
